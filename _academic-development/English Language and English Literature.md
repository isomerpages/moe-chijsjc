<h4><strong>Our Objectives</strong></h4>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To cultivate empowered and engaged 21st century learners who are discerning
yet empathetic and effective communicators</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To inspire a deep appreciation for the English Language and Literature,
and their use in creative expression and in daily life</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h4><strong>Our Approach</strong></h4>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Focus on experiential learning and guided discovery to allow for deep
learning</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Explicit instruction on the use of visible thinking strategies to enhance
metacognition</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Opportunities to harness ICT to allow for the co-construction of knowledge</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h4><strong>Our Programmes</strong></h4>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Experience the authentic use of English Language and Literature through
theatre experiences and learning beyond the classroom</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Explore different perspectives of the world through dramatisation and
debates</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Engage in dialogic classroom discussion to develop richer perspectives</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Through all the above programmes, students will learn to use the English
Language with impact, effect and affect.</p>
