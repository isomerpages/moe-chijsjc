<h4><strong>About</strong></h4>
<p>The CHIJ SJC Angklung &amp; Kulintang Ensemble “Plays Together, Shakes
as One”.&nbsp; The CCA is made up of a team of young and talented individuals
who play a range of instruments, including several angklungs on a rack
at the same time, to create harmonic musical pieces.</p>
<p>Members are expected to uphold the standards established to produce high
quality music during practice sessions &amp; performances. These experiences
emphasise the development of discipline, commitment, confidence, teamwork
and care for each other.&nbsp; Together, the Angklung family strives to
give our best to every performance as we work hard and cultivate a passion
for the CCA.</p>
<h4><strong>Objectives</strong></h4>
<ul>
<li>
<p>To nurture in members an appreciation for the performing arts through
the development and application of skills of angklung and kulintang as
a musical instrument.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To develop students’ leadership potential, discipline, confidence, resilience
and team spirit through training sessions and performances.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h4><strong>Activities</strong></h4>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Competitions (e.g. Singapore Youth Festival Arts Presentation)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Performing for Internal and External Events</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Developing musicianship of each performer</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Team Building Games</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Circle Sessions for Reflection</p>
</li>
</ul>
