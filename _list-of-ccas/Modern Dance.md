<h4><strong>About</strong></h4>
<p>Life as a member in SJC's Modern Dance CCA is always filled with excitement!
SJC dancers are never short of opportunities to showcase their talents
on various platforms both in school and outside. Every year, our dancers
are actively involved in numerous school events, productions and competitions.
We are also invited to collaborate with other organisations to put up public
performances.</p>
<h4><strong>Objectives</strong></h4>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To provide enriching and meaningful programmes for the development of
its members.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To inculcate values of perseverance, self-discipline, commitment and team-work
through participation in competitions and external performances.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h4><strong>Activities</strong></h4>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Weekly training involving basic contemporary dance techniques, improvisation,
partner work, and learning choreography</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>CCA bonding sessions, Secondary 1 Welcome Party, Secondary 4 Farewell
Party</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>School performances (E.g. Teachers’ Day, Open House)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Biennial Singapore Youth Festival Arts Presentation</p>
</li>
</ul>
