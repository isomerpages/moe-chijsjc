<div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/CCA/2023/SJAB/st%20john_s%20ambulance%20brigade.jpg"></div><h4><strong>About</strong></h4><p>In St John Brigade (SJB), cadets participate in three main components: first aid and life support, home nursing and foot drill. We aim to develop cadets holistically with a strong emphasis on values and character development, as well as leadership. The first aid knowledge that cadets learn goes on to serve them well as a life skill, sparkling an interest in some to pursue medicine or nursing as their career.</p><p>To forge stronger ties as a corps, regular bonding sessions and activities are held and cadets are encouraged to participate in the Cadet Proficiency Badge Scheme to support their development beyond SJB.</p><h4><strong>Objectives</strong></h4><ul data-tight="true" class="tight"><li><p>To equip our cadets to be a competent trained first-aider to render First Aid and Home Nursing to those in need through life-saving skills of first aid, nursing, and emergency assistance.</p></li><li><p>To ensure each cadet attains proficiency in First Aid, Home Nursing and Foot Drill.</p></li><li><p>To provide a meaningful holistic 4-year CCA experience through purposeful and engaging activities.</p></li><li><p>To develop leadership skills, independence, team building skills, resilience and good character.</p></li><li><p>To provide every cadet with the opportunity to graduate with the Chief Commissioner's Badge, the highest level of achievement for a a cadet.</p></li></ul><h4><strong>Activities</strong></h4><p>We engage our cadets with fun and enriching CCA sessions, enabling them to perform basic first aid and life-saving skills.</p><ul data-tight="true" class="tight"><li><p>Basic / Advanced Non-Commissioner Officer Course (BNCOC / ANCOC)</p></li><li><p>Cadet Proficiency Badge Scheme (CPBS)</p></li><li><p>Hospital Attachment Programme</p></li><li><p>Public duties as first-line first-aiders at school and community events</p></li><li><p>Zonal Nursing and First Aid Competitions</p></li></ul><h4><strong>CCA Information</strong></h4><p><strong>Schedule</strong><br>Friday: 3.45pm -
