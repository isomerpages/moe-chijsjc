<h4>About</h4>
<p>The Vox Collective presents a unique opportunity for students to develop
their oratorical skills through CCA. As a CCA, we provide a vital platform
for students to practice expressing their thoughts clearly, articulately,
and persuasively. During weekly sessions, participants are introduced to
fundamental debate and speechcraft techniques, with training exercises
designed to cultivate adaptive thinking and improve quick-witted improvisation.
The sessions naturally challenge students to step beyond their comfort
zones and practice crafting logical arguments, often involving in-depth
discussions about current affairs and relevant social issues to foster
a broader understanding of the world. Beyond formal speaking, students
acquire valuable soft skills, including improved time-management and communication
abilities, which contribute to the development of self-confidence. Ultimately,
The Vox Collective aims to be a supportive community where students learn
the value of open-mindedness and mutual respect while having opportunities
to bond and practice essential life skills.</p>
