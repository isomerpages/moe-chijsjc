<h4><strong>About</strong></h4>
<p>In SJC Volleyball, the players’ commitment to the rigorous training strengthen
their bond as a team. Team building activities, training camps and sports
psychology sessions all serve to strengthen the players’ mental strength.
Opportunities to spar with players from other schools are all part of the
training in this exciting sport. After a year of hard work and training,
the players will participate in the North Zone Inter-Schools ‘C’ and ‘B’
Division Girls Volleyball Championships as well as the Volleyball Association
of Singapore’s Under-14 and Under-16 competitions.</p>
<h4><strong>Objectives</strong></h4>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To develop disciplined individuals who are geared towards achieving their
goals through perseverance, resilience and determination.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To inculcate in players the core values of excellence, fair play and respect.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h4><strong>Activities</strong></h4>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Annual competition at the National School Games North Zone Inter-Schools
Volleyball Championship for ‘B’ and ‘C’ Division girls teams</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Volleyball Association of Singapore’s Under-14 and Under-16 competitions</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>End of year training camp</p>
</li>
</ul>
