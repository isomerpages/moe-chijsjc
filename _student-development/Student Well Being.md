<p>CHIJ SJC takes great pride pride in our supportive and caring culture.
School programmes are planned with the girls’ well-being and social-emotional
needs in mind. All staff, as well as a dedicated team of School Counsellors,
Special Educational Needs Officer )SENO) and Student Welfare Officer (SWO)
are there to guide and offer support for students in need.</p>
<h4><strong>Student Support Team</strong></h4>
<p>The Student Support Team comprises the School Counsellors, Special Education
Needs Officer (SENO), Student Welfare Officer (SWO) and Education and the
Career Guidance (ECG) Counsellor. They work together with teachers to provide
support required for our students to strive and thrive.</p>

<h4><strong>The Circle Room</strong></h4>
<p>The Circle Room is a dedicated space for students to self-regulate when
necessary. There are drawing materials, games and books for students’ use
as they take a time out before returning to class.</p>

<h3><strong>GEAR-UP</strong></h3>
<p>GEAR-UP stands for <u>G</u>uiding and <u>E</u>mpowering students for <u>A</u>ffiliation
and <u>R</u>esilience to <u>U</u>nlock their <u>P</u>otential. This is a programme
organised by CHIJ SJC for selected students to:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Build positive relationships and a sense of belonging,</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Provide a safe and supportive environment to build resilience and school
readiness,</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Avail opportunities for service and learning to nurture students’ strengths
and potential.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Some of the programmes organized in 2025 include sports and art workshops,
study skills lessons as well as outings e.g., Snow City.</p>
