<h4>Release of 2025 GCE-N Level Results</h4>
<p>Dear Parents and SJCians,</p>
<p>The N-Level results will be released on Thursday, 18 December 2025, at
2.00pm.</p>
<p>Please note the following arrangements during collection of the GCE N-Level
Results:</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Students are to be in their school uniform.
<br>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students and accompanying parent(s)/ guardian(s) are to be seated in the
Hall by 1.30 pm.
<br>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students who are unable to collect their results in person may arrange
for their parent/guardian to collect the results on their behalf, or appoint
a proxy (aged 21 and above) to collect a physical copy of their results
from the school. Please inform your FT if you are arranging for a proxy
to collect your results on your behalf.
<br>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>For appointment of proxy:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Please fill in the details of the proxy in the attached proxy form <a href="https://drive.google.com/file/d/1w4M4R-Lreb6ucvQAA5dikTWsBeTJV62u/view?usp=sharing" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://drive.google.com/file/d/1w4M4R-Lreb6ucvQAA5dikTWsBeTJV62u/view?usp=sharing</a>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The person whom you authorize to collect your result slip and other documents
on your behalf, must bring along the proxy form duly completed and signed
as well as his/her NRIC/ FIN/ card for verification purposes.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Please note that parent/ guardian/ authorized personnel can only collect
the results from 3.00 pm on the day of results release from the General
Office.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Parents/ Guardians are advised to park at the nearby HDB carparks.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p></p>
