<p>Dear SJCians and Parents/Guardians of the 2025 Graduating Cohort,</p>
<p>MOE and SEAB have earlier announced that the GCE O-Level Examination results
will be released on <strong>Wednesday</strong>, <strong>14 January 2026 </strong>at <strong>2.00 pm</strong>.</p>
<p><strong><u>Please note the following arrangements:</u></strong>
</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Students are to be properly groomed and dressed in their school uniform.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students and accompanying parent(s)/ guardian(s) are to be seated in the
Hall by 1.40 pm.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students who are unable to collect their results in person may arrange
for their parent/guardian to collect the results on their behalf, or appoint
a proxy (aged 21 and above) to collect a physical copy of their results
from the school. Please inform your FT if you are arranging for a proxy
to collect your results on your behalf.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>For appointment of proxy:</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>a) Please fill in the details of the proxy in the attached proxy form
<a href="https://drive.google.com/file/d/1w4M4R-Lreb6ucvQAA5dikTWsBeTJV62u/view?usp=sharing" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://drive.google.com/file/d/1w4M4R-Lreb6ucvQAA5dikTWsBeTJV62u/view?usp=sharing</a>
</p>
<p>b) The person whom you authorize to collect your result slip and other
documents on your behalf, must bring along the proxy form duly completed
and signed as well as his/her NRIC/ FIN/ card for verification purposes.</p>
<ol start="5" data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Please note that parent/ guardian/ authorized personnel can only collect
the results from 3.00 pm on the day of results release from the General
Office.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Parents/ Guardians are advised to park at the nearby HDB carparks.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>All the best to SJCians.</p>
